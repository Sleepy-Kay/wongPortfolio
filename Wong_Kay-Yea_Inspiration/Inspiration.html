Hello! My name is Kay

I'm passionate about design so here are these inspirations that help me go through my process and art!

Gharliera

An artist I've been following since my freshmen year of high school is an artist named Gharliera. A Korean digital artist who creates beautiful women in Japanese-style cyber-punk clothing. Many of his works are aesthetic, beautifully grotesque, and pretty much badass.



Eve

He makes it a whole mission to keep his face a mystery and produces such musical pieces that also have the craziest ongoing story that he's also written. Many of the days I spend listening to his music while I go to school, draw, or even gaming. He keeps me awake and honestly, I couldn't thank him more for also writing lyrics that I almost always relate to.



Danganronpa

A franchise that recently ended is a horror mystery visual novel in which you get to play a character who solves cases. It's such a big influence in my life discovering this game series and I couldn't thank it more for also influencing who I am today. Also because of the fact of how wacky each character can be but how beautiful the art can be. The art can be described as pop-up 2d art with it also having a splash of horror aspects of "who did it?"
